Alternative Metal
Alternative metal is a genre of heavy metal that gained popularity in the early 1990s and combines Heavy Metal with Alternative Rock. Most notably, alternative metal bands are characterized by heavy guitar riffs; typically, these riffs have a pronounced experimental edge, including unconventional lyrics, odd time signatures, more syncopation than typical metal, unusual technique, a resistance to conventional approaches to heavy music and an incorporation of a wide range of influences outside of the metal music scene.
