Deathgrind
Deathgrind (sometimes written as death-grind or death/grind) is a musical genre that fuses death metal and grindcore. The genre, along with pornogrind, is related to the goregrind subgenre of grindcore. Zero Tolerance described deathgrind as "grindcore and brutal death metal colliding head on." Danny Lilker described deathgrind as "combining the technicality of death metal with the intensity of grindcore."
