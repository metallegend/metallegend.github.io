Brutal Death
Brutal death metal is a subgenre of death metal which emphasises complex and fast drumming, palm muted guitar, and extremely guttural vocals. The beginnings of this movement emerged in the late 1980s with groups producing a music mixing the melodic structure of grindcore and brutality of death metal. The result is a kind of death metal with an even harsher sound. The lyrical themes of brutal death metal often invoke slasher film-stylized violence. Brutal Death Metal elaborates on the details of extreme acts, including mutilation, dissection, torture, rape and necrophilia.
